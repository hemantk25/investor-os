How this works

  1. A free Telegram bot is created once (via BotFather — takes ~3 minutes).
  2. Every morning at 7:00 AM the AI reads your portfolio + your one-pager + overnight news, and sends the brief to your phone.
  3. Price and news alerts fire during market hours only for things that touch your holdings — no noise.
  4. You reply to the bot to ask follow-up questions; answers use your full investor profile.

No app to build — Telegram is free and already on the phone. WhatsApp is possible later via the Business API.

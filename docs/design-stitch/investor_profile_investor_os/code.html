explore North Star

To build generational wealth through concentrated, high-conviction investments in foundational technologies and resilient businesses. The goal is absolute compound growth over a decade, ignoring short-term market volatility and macro-economic noise. Capital preservation is achieved through deep structural understanding, not broad diversification.

account_tree Core Philosophy

  • check_circle Concentration over Diversification: A portfolio of 8-12 deeply researched names is preferred over 50 superficially understood ones. Diversification is often a hedge against ignorance.
  • check_circle Long-Term Horizon: Minimum holding period is evaluated in 5-year increments. We buy businesses, not trading symbols.
  • check_circle Asymmetric Upside: Seek opportunities where the downside is strictly quantified and the upside is virtually uncapped due to structural moats or network effects.

gavel Mindset Rules

  1. 1
    Invert, Always Invert Before asking "how can this succeed?", rigorously define "how does this fail?". If the failure modes are acceptable or highly improbable, proceed.
  2. 2
    Ignore the Noise Do not react to daily price actions, geopolitical headlines, or analyst upgrades/downgrades. React only to fundamental changes in the business thesis.
  3. 3
    Embrace Inactivity The hardest work is done during research. Once allocated, the primary job is to sit still and let compounding do the heavy lifting. Activity is the enemy of returns.
